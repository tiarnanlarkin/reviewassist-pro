Example Request

curl -X GET "https://api.reviewassist.pro/v1/reviews" \
  -H "X-API-Key: ra_your_api_key_here" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json"

Available Endpoints

GET /api/v1/reviews
Get reviews

Retrieve reviews with optional filtering by platform, rating, and date range.

GET /api/v1/analytics/summary
Get analytics

Get analytics summary including total reviews, average rating, and platform breakdown.

POST /api/v1/reviews/{id}/respond
Respond to review

Generate and post a response to a specific review.
